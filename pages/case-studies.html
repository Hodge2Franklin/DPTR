Quest Apartments: Transforming Guest Experience

Client: Quest Apartments
Industry: Hospitality
Timeline: 12 months

The Challenge

Quest Apartments, with over 170 properties across Australia, New Zealand, and Fiji, was struggling with fragmented guest data across multiple property management systems, booking platforms, and customer touchpoints. This fragmentation made it impossible to create a unified view of guests, resulting in inconsistent experiences, missed personalization opportunities, and limited ability to drive loyalty and repeat bookings.

Key challenges included:

  • Disparate data across 170+ properties with different management systems
  • Inability to recognize returning guests across properties
  • Limited insights into guest preferences and behaviors
  • Inconsistent guest experiences across the portfolio
  • Inefficient marketing and loyalty programs due to data silos

Our Approach

We applied our human-centered data methodology to understand the guest journey from both the guest and property perspective, identifying key moments where data could enhance the experience.

  1. Deep Immersion: We conducted extensive interviews with guests, property managers, and staff to understand the current experience and pain points.
  2. Problem Mapping: We created detailed maps of the guest journey and identified critical data gaps and opportunities.
  3. Data Strategy: We designed a unified guest data platform that could integrate with all existing property management systems.
  4. Solution Design: We created a guest recognition system, preference engine, and personalization platform.
  5. Implementation: We deployed the solution across all properties with a phased approach.
  6. Continuous Evolution: We established metrics and feedback loops to continuously improve the system.

The Solution

We developed a comprehensive Guest Experience Platform that unified data across all properties and touchpoints, creating a single view of each guest regardless of which properties they visited.

Key components included:

  • Unified Guest Profile: A central repository of guest information, preferences, and history
  • Cross-Property Recognition: System to identify returning guests across any property
  • Preference Engine: AI-powered system to identify and track guest preferences
  • Personalization Platform: Tools for staff to deliver personalized experiences
  • Analytics Dashboard: Insights into guest behavior, satisfaction, and loyalty

The Impact

Beyond the numbers, the platform transformed how Quest Apartments understands and serves its guests. Staff now have the tools and insights to create meaningful, personalized experiences that build lasting relationships with guests.
